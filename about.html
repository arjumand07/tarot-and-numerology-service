About Sarah Thompson

Sarah Thompson is a seasoned tarot and numerology reader with over a decade of experience guiding individuals on their personal journeys. Her fascination with the mystical arts began in her early twenties when she stumbled upon an old tarot deck at a flea market. Intrigued by the symbols and stories woven into each card, Sarah felt an immediate connection and began her journey of exploration and study. Over the years, Sarah has delved deeply into both tarot and numerology, earning certifications from renowned institutions and studying under respected mentors. Her practice is grounded in a blend of traditional wisdom and modern insights, offering clients a unique and holistic approach to personal growth and self-discovery. Sarah’s career has flourished as she’s built a reputation for her intuitive clarity and compassionate guidance. She’s hosted workshops, written articles for spiritual magazines, and provided private readings to clients around the world. Her ability to integrate tarot insights with numerological analysis allows her to offer a comprehensive perspective on life’s questions, helping individuals to navigate their paths with confidence and clarity. Passionate about sharing her knowledge, Sarah continues to inspire and educate through her work, creating a space where individuals can explore their inner landscapes and uncover the potential within.

We are dedicated to helping our clients connect with their highest selves, gain deeper self-awareness, and embrace their unique life purpose. Our goal is to support each individual in their quest for fulfillment, balance, and inner peace, ultimately enriching their lives and empowering their personal journey
